Usage: ./run-chaos.sh <experiment-name>

Supported experiments:
  product-crash-loop
  network-delay-api-gateway
  pod-kill-product
  pod-kill-order
  cpu-stress-product

Environment overrides:
  RUN_LOAD=false
  BASELINE_DURATION=30s
  WARMUP_DURATION=60s
  CHAOS_DURATION=<override>
  RECOVERY_DURATION=60s
  LOAD_TEST_DIR=./load-testing
  LOAD_RUN_SCRIPT=<load-testing/scripts/run-user-journey.sh>
  LOAD_ENV_FILE=<load-testing/scripts-k6/.env.k6>

Examples:
  ./chaos-mesh/scripts/run-chaos.sh network-delay-api-gateway
  RUN_LOAD=true K6_WEB_DASHBOARD=true ./chaos-mesh/scripts/run-chaos.sh network-delay-api-gateway
